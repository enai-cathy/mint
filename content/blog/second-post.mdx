## Why Saving in Dollars Matters

Inflation is real. In 2024, the naira lost over 30% of its value. That means ₦1,000 last year might only buy you ₦700 worth of goods today.

If you’re earning from Upwork, remote work, or affiliate income — saving in dollars protects your wealth.
